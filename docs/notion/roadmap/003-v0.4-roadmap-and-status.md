## 🛠️ Complete Catalog of Completed Milestones

### 1. PitchMind V0.3 — "Pro-Matchup" PCI & Global Leaderboards
* **Comparative Postural Congruency Index (PCI) Algebra:** A mathematical comparative pose matching engine ([pci_calculator.py](file:///C:/Users/saipa/.gemini/antigravity/scratch/pitchmind/ai-engine/pitchmind_ai/biomechanics/pci_calculator.py)) that shifts coordinate midpoints to a pelvic center $(0,0)$ and scales heights to the player's torso length to calculate cosine angular similarity across shoulders, wrists, knees, and ankles.
* **Global REST Leaderboards:** A competitive sorting API (`GET /api/v1/leaderboards`) that joins completion status, slices the top 100 players, and groups rankings by stroke profile and grassroots location demographics.
* **Competitive Rankings Dashboard:** A glassmorphic scoreboard page featuring search, region filters, gold/silver/bronze rankings, and glowing emerald PCI badges.
* **Session Details HUD Overlays:** Live HUD scorecards projecting congruency comparisons straight onto player cards.

### 3. PitchMind V1.1 — Premium Telestrators & Calibrations

## 🎯 Proposed Roadmap & Suggestions for PitchMind V0.4

To make PitchMind a world-class training suite for professional selectors, the following **Four Core Upgrades** are recommended for the **V0.4 Release**:

### 🚀 Milestone 1: Kinematic Power & Joint Velocity Chain
* **Mathematical Concept:** Track the energy sequence transfer from the ground up (Footwork $\rightarrow$ Knees $\rightarrow$ Pelvis $\rightarrow$ Torso/Shoulders $\rightarrow$ Arms $\rightarrow$ Wrists).
* **Biomechanics Formula:** Calculates the millisecond delay between the peak angular acceleration of each joint:
  $$\Delta t = t(\text{Peak}_\text{Wrist}) - t(\text{Peak}_\text{Hip})$$
* **The Diagnostic Value:** Automatically flags "power leaks" (e.g., if a player snaps their wrists before their hip rotation peaks, cutting off power transfer) and displays this sequence on a high-fidelity visual timing grid.

### 🎴 Milestone 2: Shareable "Grassroots Scout Cards" (FUT-style)
* **What It Is:** An ultra-premium, interactive digital card compiling a player's core biomechanical metrics in a single, shareable graphic.
* **Visual Features:** A stunning glassmorphic card displaying the player's name, avatar, and grassroots location demographics alongside a **5-axis radar chart** measuring:
  1. *PCI Congruency* (Posture Match vs Pros)
  2. *Kinetic Velocity* (Bat Speed/Hip Rotation)
  3. *Balance Quotient* (COM & Stance Stability)
  4. *Injury Safety Rating* (Low Joint Strain)
  5. *Length Judgment* (Footwork Decision Time)
* **The Value:** Drives strong viral loops; allows players to export and showcase their metrics to academies, talent selectors, and scouting networks.

### 🔄 Milestone 3: Dual-Scrubber Synchronized Video Player
* **What It Is:** Real-time side-by-side video playback of player practice vs elite professional baselines.
* **How it works:** Rather than just overlaying skeletons, render a split-screen video player loading the player's practice video side-by-side with an elite professional’s video (e.g., Kohli's Cover Drive). 
* **The Value:** A single timeline scrubber scrubs **both video clips** in exact unison, locked to matched swing milestones (Setup, Backswing Peak, Impact, Follow-Through), giving players direct visual confirmation of their biomechanical alignment.

### 🎙️ Milestone 4: Voice-to-Drill AI Assistant
* **What It Is:** Hands-free voice commands designed specifically for batting net environments.
* **How it works:** A player can voice-query the dashboard: *"How do I fix my dropped front elbow?"* The voice-agent parses their history, verbally describes the correct drill (e.g., "The high-pin shadow drive") via TTS, and automatically jumps to highlight the micro-error frames on their canvas interface.
